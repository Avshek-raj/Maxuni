A Paradise for Trek lovers : Everest Base Camp Trek

January 26, 2022January 29, 2022 admin 1 Comment

Everest Base Camp trek, is one of the best treks in the world that centres on the world’s highest peak Mt. Everest (29,029 ft/ 8,848.68m). This trek (5,364m /17,598ft) will provide you with a natural thrill as it takes you through breathtaking high-altitude landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, traditional Sherpa culture, high-altitude animals, and snow-capped mountains, lakes. EBC is located at the height of(5,364m /17,598ft).

 

Highlights of Everest Base Camp Trek

  • Stay at the Base camp of Mount Everest at a height of (5,364m /17,598ft) with majestic mountain surrounding you.
  • World’s highest airport at a height of 3,780m / 12.402ft).
  • Explore the interesting culture of Local Sherpa people staying in there.
  • Witness the sun setting  over  Everest from Kalapatthar which is  also known as Eevrest viewpoint at a height of(5,555m).
  • Ancient Monastery at Tengboche (3,867m / 12,687ft).
  • Witness the beauty of Khu,mbu Glacier and Khumbu Icefalls.
  • Explore Sagarmatha national park with its wild range of flora and fauna.
  • View of other peaks like Mt. Lhotse(8,516m), Cho Oyu (8,201m) and Mt. Makalu (8,463m).

Everest Base Camp Trek Itinerary

Day 1 : Kathmandu to Lukla

             Take a flight to from Tribhuvan airport on Kathmandu to Lukla(2651 m / 8697ft) which takes around 4 hrs. Enjoy the stunning aerial  view of mountains and geographical structure.

Day 2 : Lukla to Namche Bazar(Namche – 3440 m / 11286 ft)

             Hike to Namche Bazar which may take about 5 to 6 hrs.

Day 3 :Namche Bazar 

              Acclimatization Day in Namche 

Day 4 :  Namche Bazar to Tengboche(Tengboche – 3956 m / 12979 ft)

               Hike from Namche bazar to Tengboche through woods and rhodhodendron forest which takes around 5 to 6 hrs.

Day 5 : Tengboche to Dingboche(Dingboche – 4380 m / 14370 ft )

            Hike from Tengboche to Dingboche crossing bridges and rivers with sweeping views of the mountains which takes around 5 to 6 hrs. 

Day 6 : Dingboche

                Acclimatization day in Dingboche.

Day 7 : Dingboche to Lobuche(Lobuche – 4938 m / 16200 ft)

             Hike from Dingboche to Lobuche through alpine meadows and summer yak pastures towards the end of the moraine of the Khumbu Glacier which takes around 4 to 5 hrs. 

Day 8 : Lobuche to Gorakshep (Pheriche – 4371 m / 14340 ft – 8 to 9 hrs)

                 Cross the Changri Glacier to Gorakshep, and enjoy the highlight of reaching the Everest Base Camp.

Day 9 : Gorakshep to Pheriche (Pheriche – 4371 m / 14340 ft – 8 to 9 hrs)

                Treak to Kala Pathar in the morning for stunning sunrise views of Everest and surrounding vistas. Later descend to Periche.

Day 10 : Pheriche to Namche Bazar (Namche – 3440 m / 11286 ft – 6 to 7 hrs)

               Descend through Pangboche, with its great gompa, to Kyangjuma to Namche.

Day 11 :  Namche Bazar to Lukla ( Lukla – 2651 m / 8697ft)

               Finally Descend down to Lukla.

Day 12 : Lukla to Kathmandu

                  Catch the flight back to Kathmandu.
